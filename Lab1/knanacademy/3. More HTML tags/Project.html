Garlic Butter Lemon Shrimp

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Large shrimp (peeled and deveined) 1 lb
Unsalted butter 3 tbsp
Garlic (minced) 3 cloves
Red pepper flakes (optional) 1 tsp
Lemon (juiced) 1
Fresh parsley (chopped) 2 tbsp

Step 1: Sauté Garlic: Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes; cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
Step 2: Cook Shrimp: Add shrimp in a single layer. Cook for 2 minutes per side until pink and opaque.
Step 3: Finish: Stir in lemon juice and parsley. Toss to coat and serve immediately.

5-Ingredient Pesto Tortellini

  • Time: 12 minutes
  • Time: 3-4
Ingredients Quantity
Refrigerated cheese tortellini 20 oz
Basil pesto (store-bought is fine) 1/2 cup
Cherry tomatoes (halved) 1 cup
Parmesan cheese (grated) 1/2 cup
Fresh baby spinach 2 cups

Step 1: Boil Pasta: Cook tortellini in boiling salted water according to package directions (usually 3–5 minutes).
Step 2: Wilting: Place the raw spinach in the bottom of your colander and drain the hot pasta directly over it to wilt the greens instantly.
Step 3: Toss: Return everything to the pot. Stir in pesto and tomatoes.
Step 4: Serve: Top with plenty of Parmesan cheese.

Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Salmon fillets 2
Honey 2 tbsp
Soy sauce 1 tbsp
Lemon juice 1 tbsp
Olive oil 1 tbsp
Salt & Pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Prep Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk honey, soy sauce, and lemon juice.
  2. Sear: Season salmon with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Place salmon skin-side up and sear for 5 minutes.
  3. Glaze: Flip the fillets. Pour the sauce into the pan.
  4. Reduce: Cook for another 3–4 minutes while spooning the bubbling sauce over the fish until it becomes thick and sticky.
